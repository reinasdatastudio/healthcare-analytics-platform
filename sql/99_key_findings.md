# Key Findings

## Patient Demographics

- The synthetic population contained 5764 patients
- There were 2875 males and 2889 females
- The mean age was 45 years
- Middlesex County had the most patients of 1332.

## Healthcare Utilisation

- Total of 334531 encounters were recorded
- The average number of encounters was 58.


## Disease Analysis

- Total of 207966 conditions have been recorded with 303 unique records
- Gingivitis was the most prevalent acute disorder
- Hypertension was the most prevalent chronic disease
- Diabetes prevalence was the highest for age group 40-64.

## Medication Analysis

- Lisinopril was the most frequently prescribed drug for patients with diabetes
- Insulin was the most common diabetic medication
- Lisinopril was the most common antihypertensive medication.

## Clinical Observations

- Patients with diabetes had a higher average BMI.
- There were significantly higher volume of glucose in urine excreted from non-diabetic patients
- A patient-level cardiovascular risk profile was constructed using age, BMI, diabetes and hypertension.
